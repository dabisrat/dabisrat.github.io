About Me

Hello and Welcome! My name is David Bisrat and I'm a software engineer located in Atlanta, GA.

I'm a lover of all things science, technology and gaming. Web technologies are where I spend the majority of my time right now. Like most people, I spend a ridiculous amount of time on the internet, so understanding web development and the direction it's headed in is a personal mission of mine.

Guided by the principles of TDD and agile development, my goal is to build applications in a streamlined and iterative fashion while trying to minimize debt. Technologies I currently enjoy using include React-Redux, Bable, Webpack, Bootstrap, and Node/Express.
