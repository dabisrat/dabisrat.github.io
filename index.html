Welcome

Hello World! I’d like to welcome you to my little slice of the web. I’m an Atlanta based web developer looking to build cool things with cool people. Currently, I’m looking for work while trying to take my CS skills to the next level. I just finished a grueling three-month CS Bootcamp that truly pushed me out of my comfort zone. My plan is to try and post on a regular basis (once a week) about all the cool and crazy things I discover on my road to web mastery.

Motivations

My primary motivation for writing this blog is rather selfish. One of my teachers once told me that writing your ideas down enhances the learning experience by giving physical form to your thoughts, so I figured I’d write about topics I found tricky or overly confusing in hopes that the notions would stick long term. I also hope to refine my written communication skills as it is a paramount skill in any aspects of life and no man is an Island. Lastly, I want a central repository for the work I’ve done/will do so that I can track my progression. The other reason is to share what I find. The open source community has left a significant mark on me, and I’d like to give back in any way that I can. If one of my posts illuminates your understanding of a topic or peaks your curiosity, then it was worth it IMHO.
